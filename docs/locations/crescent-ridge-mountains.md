# Crescent Ridge Mountains

The Crescent Ridge Mountains span the entire eastern coastline of the continent. The range of these mountains spans about 300 miles across at its densest point, fittingly in the middle of the range geographically. As the range extends to the north and south it becomes thinner across, though it maintains much of its vertical height. While several well traveled paths through these mountains exist, one one is maintained year round while the rest are available only when the whether permits.

The foothills to the West are protected by the [Eastern Woodlands](./eastern-woodlands.md), making the mountains a remarkebly secure backstop for the many port cities and towns that dot the Eastern coast. A small few of these coastal cities, known as the Jewels of the Ridge, are large enough to extend into the mountains proper. To the northern and southern ends of the continent the mountain terrain comes so close the water edge as to make civilized settlement impractical, these regions are known as the Northern and Southern Shelves.

## The Five Teeth

Of the many peaks that make up the Crescent Ridge, the Five Teeth stand tallest and sharpest. Rising several thousand feet above their neighbors, five peaks of this range extend up into the sky at such a steep angle that even snow struggles to cling to the hard wind-worn rock faces.

=== DM Knowledge

    The teeth are the arcane artifacts of a great working by High Sorcerer Edward Thume of the House Aketh, which lead to [Nocturne No.2](../events/nocturnes.md#nocturne-no2). Thume was leveraging the highest available mountains of the Crescent Ridge in an attempt to create a spell engine large enough to forcebly summon the Aketh into the mortal world. To do so, he needed them tuned to specific frequencies. Thume reshaped these peaks, forming the tuning mechanism to cast his spell.

    While Thume's sorcery ultimately succeeded, forcing Aketh into the mortal world effectively started [Nocturne No.2](../events/nocturnes.md#nocturne-no2). The arcane being, formerly a deity of storms, unraveled upon entering the mortal plane. Aketh's magic spread across the entirety of the world, starting from the Five Teeth, blanketing the sky in a storm that lasted for 200 years. During this storm, the teeth were struck by arcane lightening so many times that it caused the transformation of the rock surface we see today, turning the rough stone surface to a sharp glassy texture. Modern mining efforts below The Five Teeth often find evidence of the magical torment the rocks above suffered shown thousands of feet below in glassy black veins shapped like lightening that spiderweb through the mountains.
