# Kaeroto Cultural Notes

## Kaeroto Language Rules

This is the language used by the northern tribes of the Aketh Expanse. This document will need more fleshing out, but for now I just need to keep track of the language rules I'm setting.

### Suffixes

- `-k` or `-ok` after proper noun to indicate "home of."
- `-to` after a proper noun to demark a group that follows the one named.
