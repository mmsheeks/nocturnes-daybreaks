# The Silou Forest

Deep within [The Eastern Woodlands](../locations/eastern-woodlands.md) lies a forest distinct in its age and strangeness. The trees here are older than anywhere else in the world, having lived through ages that have now been lost to the memory of the other mortal races. The trees remember. It is said that this place is the home of spirits, or demons, or ghosts — depends on who's telling the story. Whatever the case, The Silou Forest is known to all who have heard of it as a place of strange happenings and dangerous magics.
