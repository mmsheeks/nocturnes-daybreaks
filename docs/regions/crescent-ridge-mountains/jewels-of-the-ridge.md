## Batroz: Jewel of the North

Sitting at the head of a wide inlet that is surrounded on all sides by steep mountains at the Eastern edge of the [Northern Shelf](./northern-shelf.md) is the fortress city of Batroz.

A large and sprawling dockyard sits on the water, leading up the shore to massive gates in an even more imposing [arcsteel](../../asides/magical-materials.md#arcsteel) wall. Beyond the wall lies a bustling city full of tall metal and stone buildings that set themselves all the way back into the side of the mountain. These towering structures are lit by a warm dancing light that pours up the walls from the busy markets below.

Batroz is a city known by two universally understood truths. The towering smithies and mines dug into these mountain faces are the source of the finest metals one can buy, and everything comes at a price.

## Waifin: Jewel of the East

Calling Waifin the Jewel of the East is, perhaps, a bit of a stretch. Its recognition by the other two cities is conditional, at best. Not to mention given the geography of the area, Waifin is barely any farther East than either of the others. None the less, the city claims the title and is recognized for it.

Located at the farthest possible edge of the continent, past the eastern barrier mountains of the [Aketh Expanse](./aketh-expanse.md), sits the former fishing town of Waifin. Accessible only by sea, or by traveling a long and dangerous path through the eastern barrier mountains, Waifin has grown in size considerably the last several generations.

What lead to its rise was the establishment of the first adventuring guild outpost here. That first guild made it known that any adventurer who could reach Waifin by foot would be automatically granted the highest adventuring rank in their guild. The rest of the guilds soon followed, and Waifin became the defacto adventuring capitol of the world.

Most adventuring guilds keep a chapter in Waifin to this day, though none have their headquarters located in Waifin. Instead, the city is considered neutral ground. Adventurers who make it here are offered reward both in rank, and in access to generous contracts and gear that can't be found anywhere else in the world.
