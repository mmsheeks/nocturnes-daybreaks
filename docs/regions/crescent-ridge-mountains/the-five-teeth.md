# The Five Teeth

Of the many peaks that make up the Crescent Ridge, the Five Teeth stand tallest and sharpest. Rising several thousand feet above their neighbors, five peaks of this range extend up into the sky at such a steep angle that even snow struggles to cling to the hard wind-worn rock faces.

=== "DM Knowledge"

    The teeth are the arcane artifacts of a great working by High Sorcerer Edward Thume of the House Aketh, which lead to [Nocturne No.2](../../events/nocturnes.md#nocturne-no2). Thume was leveraging the highest available mountains of the Crescent Ridge in an attempt to create a spell engine large enough to forcibly summon Aoukethiir into the mortal world. To do so, he needed them tuned to specific frequencies. Thume reshaped these peaks, forming the tuning mechanism to cast his spell.

    While Thume's sorcery ultimately succeeded, forcing Aoukethiir into the mortal world effectively started [Nocturne No.2](../../events/nocturnes.md#nocturne-no2). The arcane being, formerly a deity of storms, unraveled upon entering the mortal plane. Aoukethiir's magic spread across the entirety of the world, starting from the Five Teeth, blanketing the sky in a storm that lasted for 200 years. During this storm, the teeth were struck by arcane lightening so many times that it caused the transformation of the rock surface we see today, turning the rough stone surface to a sharp glassy texture. Modern mining efforts below The Five Teeth often find evidence of the magical torment the rocks above suffered shown thousands of feet below in glassy black veins shaped like lightening that spiderweb through the mountains.
