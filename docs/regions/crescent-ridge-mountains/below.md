# The Below

!!! danger "SPOILERS"

    Everything in this document is a spoiler unless your character has a VERY specific back story. I would have told you. Read at your own risk.

The Below is a vast expanse of deep cavernous valleys beneath the [Aketh Expanse](./aketh-expanse.md) accessible only through a select few entrances left behind by [House Aketh](../../groups/great-houses.md#house-aketh) during the [Second Daybreak](../../events/daybreaks.md#second-daybreak).
