# The Eastern Woodlands

Just West of the [Crescent Ridge Mountains](../crescent-ridge-mountains/index.md) on the Eastern side of the continent lies a vast expanse of forested land. These forests are known collectively as the Eastern Woodlands. Made up of a mix between untamed wilderness, some mundane and some magical, and inhabited lands that have been developed to different degrees and in differing traditions, the Eastern Woodlands span nearly the entire breadth of the continent from North to South.

Due to the sheer scope of land the forest occupies, it contains a variety of different ecological and arcane zones. These distinct forests often blend with each other near their edges, though some have a distinct boundary.
