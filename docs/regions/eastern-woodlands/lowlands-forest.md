# Lowlands Forest

These forests are more sparse, sometimes giving way to open fields that are surrounded on all sides by tree cover. Made up largely of shorter and relatively young birch-like trees, the ground here is rich with fresh flora and critters finding plenty of refuge in recently fallen trunks.

The lowlands forests, being in much more level and hospitable land, are also the most densely inhabited. [Mira](../../groups/mira.md) has a few small outpost villages in the forest, which serve as trade hubs and safe haven for those traveling this far East from the Empire. The independent city-state of [Hygrin](./hygrin.md) also sits within these lands, and is home to one of the older noble houses.
